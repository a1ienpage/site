About Us

MNU Data Science Club is the first tech-oriented club at Maqsut Narikbayev University, dedicated to popularizing IT in Business and Economics, as well as Data Science disciplines.


We aim to bridge the gap between technical skills and business applications, providing students with hands-on experience and networking opportunities in the rapidly evolving field of data science.


Our club offers workshops, competitions, industry talks, and collaborative projects that allow members to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problems.

Club Structure

Our club is organized into two main departments, each with specialized divisions to cover all aspects of data science and club management.

Technical Department

Focuses on developing technical skills and knowledge in data science, programming, and mathematical foundations.

Programming Division

Python, R, SQL, and other programming languages essential for data science.

Mathematics Division

Mathematical foundations, algorithms, and computational methods.

Statistics Division

Statistical analysis, probability theory, and data interpretation.

Social Department

Handles the community aspects of the club, including outreach, event planning, and membership management.

Human Resources Division

Member recruitment, onboarding, and community building.

Surveys Division

Conducting research, gathering feedback, and analyzing club performance.

Events Division

Planning workshops, hackathons, competitions, and social gatherings.
